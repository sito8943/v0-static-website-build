Who We Are

We’re a small team of roasters, bakers, and hosts who believe coffee is best enjoyed in bright, welcoming spaces. Every detail—beans, water, pastry, playlist—is chosen to lift your mood.

Solstice began as a weekend pop-up and grew into a neighborhood spot where people read, write, and reconnect. We can’t wait to welcome you.
